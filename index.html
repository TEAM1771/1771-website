Meet our team!

ABOUT US
Team 1771 is a student-led competitive robotics team representing North Gwinnett High School in FRC. (What's FRC?)

OUR MISSION
To harness our team’s dedication, creativity, and cooperation to build the best product that we can, inspiring our students to be the next generation of engineers, programmers, and managers.

WHAT WE DO
In our program, our members learn 3D design, metalworking, woodworking, programming, graphic design, team management, financial planning, and machining. Team 1771 is a three-time state finalist competitive robotics team from North Gwinnett High School. We are a group of students with a passion for engineering, programming, design, and STEM leadership. In our program, our members learn 3D design, metalworking, woodworking, programming, graphic design, team management, financial planning, and machining.
If you choose to sponsor us, not only are you helping your local community and the next generation of American engineers, you also will receive tangible benefits. As a veteran team, we will publicize your company at all of our competitions.

​Because we are a non-profit organization, we have a 501(c)(3) status, meaning that any donations are tax-exempt. In-kind donations are also appreciated, and will be properly recognized. Every donation helps, and no donation is too small!

Our team cannot survive without the help of its sponsors. Registration for our first two competitions is $5,000. The Peachtree Championship has an additional attendance cost of $4,000, and the Worlds Championship fee is $5,000. We also have to purchase materials to build our robot, arrange lodging during competitions, and maintain our tools. Our target budget typically is around $20,000 overall.
